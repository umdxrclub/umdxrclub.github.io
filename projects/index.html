Throughout the year, XR Club members collaborate on group projects to apply what they've learned in workshops and bring their ideas to life. Join our Discord server for more information on currently active projects and challenges!

PROJECT SHOWCASE

VIRTUAL REALITY REAL TIME STRATEGY GAME

With this project we are creating a real time strategy (RTS) game which will be played in virtual reality (VR). We hope to take advantage of the great immersion and unique control scheme of VR to create a unique RTS experience.

AUGMENTED REALITY STORYBOOK

Turns regular storybooks/comic strip panels into AR/VR products. Goal of this project is to help encourage reading while also making it interactable, especially for young children.

CHALLENGES

NASA S.U.I.T.S. CHALLENGE

PROPOSALS DUE NOVEMBER 1 2022

NASA S.U.I.T.S (Spacesuit User Interface Technologies for Students) is an annual challenge for undergraduate students to design and build spacesuit information displays in augmented reality.

YEARS SUBMITTED PROPOSAL: 2021, 2022

SNAP CREATIVE CHALLENGE

PROPOSALS DUE JULY 31 2022

The Snap Creative Challenge is an opportunity for HCI and AR researchers to design immersive and multisensorial experiences beyond looking at images and videos through a phone.

YEARS SUBMITTED PROPOSAL: 2021, 2022
